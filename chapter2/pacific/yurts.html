The Yurts of Pacific Trails Resort

What is a Yurt?
A luxury yurt blends adventure with indulgence—think plush bedding, a crackling fireplace, and spa-like amenities wrapped in a stylish, weatherproof retreat.
With panoramic windows, skylights, and private decks, it’s the perfect way to immerse yourself in nature without sacrificing comfort.
Ready to escape in style?
How are the yurts finished?
Each of our yurts features a private deck, hand-painted furniture, electric heating, and indoor plumbing.
With luxury finishes and soaring ceilings, you’ll be inspired to explore by day and relax in style by night.
What should I pack?
Most guests find that they pack too much! We provide all bedding, towels, and toiletries.
Be sure to bring sturdy shoes for exploring the trails, and a sense of adventure.
