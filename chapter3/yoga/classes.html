Find Your Inner Light

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Discover balance and serenity with Gentle Hatha Yoga, a calming practice designed for all levels. This class focuses on slow, mindful movements and gentle stretches to improve flexibility, strength, and relaxation. Perfect for beginners or anyone seeking a more relaxed pace, Gentle Hatha Yoga emphasizes breath awareness and the connection between mind, body, and spirit. Whether you're looking to reduce stress or enhance your overall well-being, this practice offers a peaceful space to unwind, recharge, and find inner harmony.
Vinyasa Yoga
Elevate your practice with Vinyasa Yoga, a dynamic and flowing style that links breath with movement. This invigorating class encourages seamless transitions between poses, creating a rhythmic flow that builds strength, flexibility, and endurance. Suitable for all levels, Vinyasa Yoga challenges both the body and mind while promoting mindfulness and breath control. Whether you're looking to build energy, release tension, or deepen your practice, Vinyasa offers a powerful way to center yourself and find flow both on and off the mat.
Restorative Yoga
Experience deep relaxation and healing with Restorative Yoga, a gentle practice designed to restore both body and mind. Using props like blankets, bolsters, and blocks, each pose is held for an extended period to encourage complete relaxation and release of tension. Perfect for those seeking stress relief, recovery, or a peaceful retreat, Restorative Yoga promotes restorative benefits by calming the nervous system and improving flexibility. This nurturing practice offers a peaceful space to unwind, reconnect, and restore balance to your life.
